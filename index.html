Other Ways to Give

Choose the method that works best for you.

Bequest Intention

Leaving a gift to WXPN in your will or estate plan is a lasting way to secure the future of music discovery and artist-driven radio for the next generation.

Life Insurance Policy

Naming WXPN as a beneficiary of a life insurance policy or annuity is a simple way to make a meaningful future gift that costs nothing today.

IRA Gifts

If you're 70½ or older, direct a Qualified Charitable Distribution (QCD) from your IRA to WXPN—it satisfies your RMD and isn't counted as taxable income.

Charitable Gift Annuity

Contribute cash or securities and receive fixed income for life. After your lifetime, remaining funds support WXPN—a win-win for you and the station.

Trusts and Real Estate

Gifts of appreciated property, charitable remainder trusts, or real estate can provide tax benefits and lasting support for WXPN while preserving your legacy.

Give by Check

Make checks payable to WXPN and mail to: WXPN Member Services, 3905 Spruce Street, Philadelphia, PA 19104.

Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and checks. For checks, please mail to WXPN Member Services, 3905 Spruce Street, Philadelphia, PA 19104.

What's the difference between one-time and sustaining membership?

A sustaining membership is an ongoing monthly or annual gift that renews automatically, providing dependable funding for WXPN. One-time donations are single contributions that you'll be asked to renew later. Sustainers are especially valuable because they provide the most reliable support.

How do I change or cancel my membership?

To update your payment information or cancel your sustaining membership, please contact Member Services at members@xpn.org or 215-898-6677. We're here Monday–Friday, 9am–5pm ET. You can cancel your sustaining gift at any time.

When will I receive my thank-you gift?

Thank-you gifts and membership premiums typically arrive within 4–8 weeks of your donation. If you have questions about your gift status, please contact Member Services.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! WXPN is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization under the University of Pennsylvania. Your donation is tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. We'll send you an annual tax receipt for your records.

Does my membership ever expire?

Annual memberships need to be renewed each year to maintain your benefits. Sustaining members remain active until they choose to cancel. We'll send you renewal reminders before your annual membership expires.
